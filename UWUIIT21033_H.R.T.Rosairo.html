Aquarium hobbyists have a passion for keeping fish and other aquatic life in a home aquarium. They enjoy learning about the different species, tank maintenance, and the joy of watching their fish swim and interact with each other. Aquarium hobbyists typically spend time researching the best tank setup, water chemistry, and the type of fish that will thrive in their aquarium. They also need to purchase the right supplies such as aquariums, water pumps, filters, and lighting. As the hobbyist progresses, they may want to add decorations and other items to enhance their aquarium experience.

What sort of fish do you keep? is one of the first questions we ask guests at Aquarium Fish Depot. That is how we intend to begin a relationship that will last a lifetime—not just with us, but also with the pastime and fine art of fishkeeping. Once you master the fundamentals, it truly is an artistic endeavor.

You’ve probably heard the saying, “Give a man a fish and you feed him for a day. Teach a man to fish and you feed him for a lifetime.” For us, it goes, “Give a person a fish and they enjoy it for a day, teach them to raise fish and they become a lifelong hobbyist.” If you ask an aquarist how can they spend hours just staring at the underwater life in their aquarium, they will answer that there is something magical about admiring floating fish and waving plants. They won’t be lying.

Dont worry. We'll help you while you dive into the world of fishkeeping!
